# Jobs And Requirements

Jobs are units of work for a site. Requirements describe what is needed to
complete a Job.

## Job States

- Unassigned Jobs are not yet planned into a Site Visit.
- Assigned Jobs are linked to a Site Visit on an active Trip.
- Completed Jobs require a completed date.
- Cancelled Jobs require a closeout note.

When a Job is assigned to a Site Visit, the Trip workflow owns assignment and
closeout fields. Normal Job editing can still update planning metadata while the
Trip remains active.

## Requirements

Requirements can represent tools, parts, cables, consumables, permissions,
notes, or items already at site. Active Jobs can have Requirements added,
edited, deleted, and confirmed.

Completed and cancelled Jobs freeze Requirement structure and confirmation.
