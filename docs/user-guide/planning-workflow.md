# Planning Workflow

Planning starts with sites from the external feed and ends with Trip closeout.

## Prepare Work

Create Job Templates for repeated work. Templates can include Template
Requirements so new Jobs start with the expected tools, parts, permissions, and
notes.

Create Work Programmes when a batch of Jobs shares a planning period or due
date. Jobs can also exist outside a Work Programme.

## Plan A Trip

A Trip defines a field deployment with dates, a Trip Leader, team members,
approval state, and notes.

Add Site Visits to record the sites that will be attended during the Trip. A
Site Visit can have a planned day or planned start and end time. Assigned Jobs
must belong to the same site as the Site Visit.

## Approval And Closeout

Trips can move through draft, submitted, approved, completed, or cancelled
states. Completed and cancelled Trips freeze normal editing.

Trip closeout records whether Site Visits were completed or skipped and whether
assigned Jobs were completed, cancelled, or returned to unassigned.

The detailed workflow rules are maintained in
[Workflow Policy](../workflows/workflow-policy.md).
